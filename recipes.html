Espresso Recipe
  • Freshly ground coffee beans (18g)
  • Water heated to 93°C

Pull a 25-30 second shot using an espresso machine. Serve immediately.

Iced Latte Recipe
  • 1 shot espresso
  • Ice cubes
  • Cold milk (200ml)

Brew espresso, pour over ice, add milk. Stir and enjoy chilled.

Pumpkin Spice Latte
  • 1 shot espresso
  • Milk (150ml)
  • Pumpkin spice syrup (2 tbsp)

Mix syrup and espresso, add steamed milk, top with cinnamon.

Mocha Recipe
  • 1 espresso shot
  • Steamed milk
  • 2 tbsp chocolate syrup

Blend espresso with syrup, top with milk & cocoa powder.

Affogato Recipe
  • 1 scoop vanilla ice cream
  • 1 shot espresso

Pour hot espresso directly over the ice cream. Serve immediately.

Caramel Macchiato
  • 1 shot espresso
  • Steamed milk
  • Caramel syrup & drizzle

Add caramel syrup to cup, pour milk, top with espresso and caramel drizzle.

Flat White
  • 1-2 shots espresso
  • Steamed milk with microfoam

Pour milk over espresso for a smooth, balanced texture.

Frappuccino
  • Espresso or coffee
  • Ice
  • Milk & whipped cream

Blend all and top with cream, caramel, or chocolate as desired.

Peppermint Mocha
  • 1 shot espresso
  • Milk
  • Peppermint + Chocolate syrup

Mix both syrups with espresso and milk. Top with whipped cream & candy cane.
